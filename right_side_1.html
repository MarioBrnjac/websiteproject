Daisy the Rabbit

The Doom Slayers Pet

Daisy

Daisy is the the Doom Slayer's pet bunny. In episodes 3 and 4 of the classic "Doom" it shows that the demons killed Daisy and the text "someone was gonna pay for what happened to Daisy, your pet rabbit" appears, meaning that the marine in the original game fought the demons to avenge Daisy
